My Projects

Explore my design work, where creativity meets functionality. Here are some of my favorite projects.

Project 1: Sip, Fill, and Stay Chill

This research kit focuses on water intake habits in a fun and interactive way. Participants use custom-designed bottles and activities like color-filling, ticking questions, and sticker mood boards to track their hydration. It’s all about making surveys engaging while promoting healthy water habits!

Project 2: We Are All Connected: Community-Based Conservation

This poster highlights the importance of biodiversity and how every creature, from the smallest ant to the tallest tree, is part of our shared ecosystem. With vibrant visuals and a clear message, it calls for action to protect and conserve our environment as one big family.

Project 3: Fudge Bliss: A Sweet Design Journey

This project combines custom typography and vibrant packaging to create an irresistible chocolate box. The bold, playful font adds personality, while the design highlights the indulgent and premium feel of single-origin chocolates. A perfect blend of creativity and sweetness!

Project 4: My Brush of Wonderland

"Elysian Wines: A vineyard bathed in the warm hues of a Romantic sunset, where rows of lush grapevines stretch towards the horizon, capturing the essence of nature's splendor and nostalgic charm. This scene encapsulates the Romanticism movement's celebration of beauty, emotion, and the sublime in the natural world."

Project 5: Letter Cap

A playful twist on typography. This letter cap reimagines the letter “W” as a blooming garden, combining classic letterforms with vibrant floral illustrations. It’s a perfect blend of modern design and timeless elegance.

Project 6: Letter Cap

Multifaceted Artistry: This canvas on canvas features a trio of paintings, including a serene landscape, an abstract masterpiece, and lively figures among sunflowers. Scattered pieces of jewelry, intricate paper quilling, and vibrant 3D flowers add to its eclectic charm, creating a unique fusion of traditional and modern artistic elements."
