About Me

I’m a second-year Communication Design student with a love for experimenting with colors, fonts, and layouts. My portfolio is a snapshot of my journey in design, filled with projects that reflect my creativity and passion. Whether it’s bold typography, clean layouts, or vibrant visuals, I believe design is all about telling a story—and I’m here to share mine.
